ABOUT US

We are Big Heart Pet Brands

 

You may not know us by name, but you probably know some of our brands—such as Milk-Bone®, Meow Mix® and Nature’s Recipe®. We are pet people and we know you are, too. And we have a simple mission: bring people and their pets closer together—through content that inspires new adventures, useful tools that make life easier, support for the pet-loving community, and high-quality products you can rely on. Because we know when people and pets spend time together, the world is a better, happier place.
